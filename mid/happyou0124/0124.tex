  \Delta = \dfrac{G}{2}\sum_{j} \dfrac{\Delta}{\sqrt{(\epsilon_j-\lambda)^2+\Delta^2}}\tanh(\dfrac{1}{2}\beta(\epsilon_j-\lambda))
\end{equation}
